# 30-Day Data Engineering Interview Roadmap 🚀

If you have a Data Engineering interview in 30 days, you don't have time to read 500-page textbooks. You need a sprint.

I've broken down the next 4 weeks into a high-intensity roadmap designed to get you "Interview Ready" at top-tier tech companies.

---

## 📅 WEEK 1: The SQL Foundation

**SQL is 60% of the battle. If you fail the SQL round, the rest doesn't matter.**

- **Days 1–4:** Master Window Functions (RANK, LEAD/LAG) and Recursive CTEs.
- **Days 5–7:** Solve 5 "Medium" LeetCode SQL problems daily. Focus on query optimization and execution plans.

---

## 📅 WEEK 2: The Coding Screen (Python)

**You don't need to be a Competitive Programmer. You need to be a Data Coder.**

- **Days 8–12:** Focus on Python internals—Dictionaries, Sets, List Comprehensions, and Error Handling.
- **Days 13–14:** Practice DSA patterns that actually appear in DE rounds: Two Pointers, Sliding Windows, and HashMaps.

---

## 📅 WEEK 3: The Big Data Engine (Spark/Airflow)

**This is where you show you can handle data at scale.**

- **Days 15–18:** Deep dive into Spark Architecture (Driver vs. Executor) and Shuffle operations.
- **Days 19–21:** Learn how to solve Data Skew and optimize joins (Broadcast vs. Sort-Merge).

---

## 📅 WEEK 4: System Design & Data Modeling

**The "Senior" round. This is where you prove you can build, not just code.**

- **Days 22–25:** Master Dimensional Modeling (Star vs. Snowflake) and SCD Types (1, 2, and 4).
- **Days 26–28:** System Design: CAP Theorem, Lambda vs. Kappa Architecture, and picking the right database (NoSQL vs. RDBMS).
- **Days 29–30:** Mock interviews and reviewing your own projects.

---

## 🎯 THE GOAL: 10% Theory, 90% Practice

Don't wait for the "perfect" time to start. The 30 days are going to pass anyway. You might as well spend them becoming a better engineer.
